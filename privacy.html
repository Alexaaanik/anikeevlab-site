Privacy Policy & Data Governance

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Commitment to Privacy

Anikeev Lab ("we", "us", or "the Lab") operates as an engineering bureau specializing in AI Architecture and Software Development. We treat data privacy not just as a legal requirement, but as a core engineering principle.

2. Data Collection & Usage

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services:

  1. Contact Information: Name and email provided via "Start Building" or direct inquiries.
  2. Technical Data: IP addresses and browser metadata for security and performance optimization (hosted on Vercel Edge Network).
  3. Project Data: Information shared during consultations is protected by strict internal confidentiality protocols.

3. AI & Neural Network Processing

As an AI-focused laboratory, we adhere to the following:

  1. Data Minimization: We do not use client-proprietary data to train public LLM models (OpenAI, Gemini, etc.) without explicit written consent.
  2. Zero-Retention Policy: Where possible, we implement API-based solutions with zero-data-retention headers to ensure your prompts remain private.
  3. Compliance: Our AI implementations are designed to be compliant with the EU AI Act (2026) and GDPR standards.

4. Third-Party Infrastructure

Our infrastructure relies on enterprise-grade providers:

  1. Hosting: Vercel (Global Edge Infrastructure).
  2. Code & Deployment: GitHub (Secure Repository Management).
  3. AI Providers: OpenAI / Google Cloud Vertex AI (subject to their respective Enterprise Privacy Tiers).

5. Your Rights

Under international law (GDPR/CCPA), you have the right to:

  1. Request a copy of your personal data.
  2. Request immediate deletion of all communication history.
  3. Object to any automated processing of your personal information.

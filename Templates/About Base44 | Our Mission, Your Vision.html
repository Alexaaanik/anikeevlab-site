    About Us

    Built for builders, powered by possibility. The story behind the platform that helps you build yours

    What is Base44?

    Base44 is an AI app builder that lets anyone turn ideas into working apps in minutes. Using just natural language, you can create personal tools, back-office apps, customer portals, or complete enterprise products that are ready to use.

    Our mission

    At Base44, our mission is to make it possible for anyone to turn their dream into a reality, without needing to code, hire a team, or wait around. We’ve created a space where building something new is as simple as describing it. No complexity. No gatekeepers. Just you, building.

    Founding story

    Base44 started with a hunch

    If you gave people the right starting point, and stripped away the friction of traditional software building, they’d create incredible things. 

    Base44 Founder and CEO
    Maor Shlomo

    Founder Maor Shlomo knew the magic wasn’t in lines of code, it was in the ideas behind them. So he built something new: a system where you describe what you want, and it appears.

    What followed was a blur of long nights, rapid progress and momentum fueled by the people building with him. As more builders joined in, it was clear that Base44 wasn’t just a platform, it was a movement.

    That energy caught the attention of Wix, whose vision deeply aligned with ours. Now, with their support, we’re moving even faster, doubling down on the mission to make building accessible to everyone.

    The future
